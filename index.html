Quarantine Healthy

Staying healthy in quarantine could be difficult for some of us. Many people think it is fine to stay in your house and do nothing. For those of you that struggle to stay active during quarantine, this will be a guide to help you.

For Adults

Being an Adult could be exhasting but there are simple things you can do to stay active and healthy such as:

1. Going for a walk with your children but remember to keep your distance from others

2. A simple 20 (or more) minute workout

3. Dance to music

For Kids

1. Practice playing a sport you enjoy

2. Go for a jog but remember to keep your distance from others

3. Dance to music

4. Doing a simple workout

Food

People sometimes lack in their choice of food. Certain foods must be eaten on a daily basis such as vegetables, meat, fruits, eggs, cheese, and milk. You need the right amount of proteins in your meal in order to stay healthy.
